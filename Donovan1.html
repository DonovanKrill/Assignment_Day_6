About Me

Donovan Krill was born and raised in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. He studied at the University of Alberta where he completed a Bachelor’s degree in Film and Media Studies. He continued his eduaction in Toronto, Ontario, Canada where he completed a Masters’ Degree in Media Production. After competing his education he moved from Canada to the Netherlands to work at Viacom International Media Networks. With over seven years experience he has become an expert regarding locationzation for the broadcasting industry. In his spare time, Donovan likes films, fitness and travel.
